Product Deep Dive

Document & Signing Hub

What it does

Manages every document from creation to signed storage

Documents Handled

  • Vendor Consignment Agreement (VCA)
  • Item Consignment Form (ICF)
  • Item Handover Receipts (3 types)
  • Acknowledgement of Obligation
  • Certificate of Ownership (manual CEO sign)

Flow

Document created → Click & Sign configured → Signers notified → Signed → Stored in DropBox → Available in portals

Technology Click & Sign API + Supabase + DropBox
Primary Users Everyone (different docs for different roles)

Inventory & Strongroom

What it does

Tracks every physical item — status, location, chain of custody

Key Features

  • Item registry with full lifecycle history
  • Strongroom check-in / check-out with timestamps
  • Auction tracker (status, catalog, tickets, financial summary)

  • Location tracking: Vendor → Strongroom → Buyer/Agent/Vendor

Product Deep Dive

Financial Operations

What it does

Invoicing, payment tracking, fee calculation, vendor payouts

Key Features

  • Invoice generation (release fees, storage, auction fees)
  • Payment tracking with 2-day and 7-day deadline windows
  • Automatic fee calculation (hammer price × percentage)
  • Vendor payout processing with full breakdown
  • Overdue payment escalation to Nomos

Key Calculation

Buyer Premium + Seller Premium = Revenue
Vendor Payout = Hammer Price - Seller Premium

Technology Part of Internal Ops Platform (Telos finance views)
Primary Users Telos (primary), CEO (visibility)

Product Deep Dive

Notification Engine

What it does

Orchestrates ALL communications across all channels

Channels

  • Email (transactional + internal)
  • SMS / WhatsApp (urgent customer notifications)
  • In-app notifications (internal)
  • Click & Sign (signing notifications)

Design Principle

Every notification = an event. Events are logged. Templates are versioned. Delivery is tracked.

Technology Supabase Edge Functions + Resend/Postmark + Twilio
Primary Users System-level, serves all personas
